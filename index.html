3 Destinations that welcomes Indians with Visa On Arrival Visa

  • Indonesia
  • Thailand
  • Hong Kong

Friendly and happening, cultured and historic, Thailand radiates a cool vibe which makes it a must-visit place on your bucket list. There’s so much to explore, monasteries, temples, beaches, flea markets, shopping arcades – you’ll be spoilt for choice. What’s even more cool is that Thailand offers a visa on arrival to Indian citizens which is valid for 15 days.

Home to the renowned Tanah Lot temple and its famous sunset point, Indonesia surely cracks a lot of punches when it comes to interesting sights. The fresh rice terraces, volcanoes, cascading waterfalls and the amazing people makes Indonesia – an interesting place to visit. What’s even more interesting to know is that Indian travellers do not require to collect a Visa before actually arriving in the country.

Dubbed as the gastronomic capital of Asia, Hong Kong is known for its iconic buildings, world class theme parks and a vibrant culture that is noticeable at every nook and corner. Apart from its cultural and architectural marvels, Hong Kong also boasts a Visa on Arrival policy that enable Indians for a 14 day visa-free stay, provided, you complete their pre-arrival registration as new rules.
